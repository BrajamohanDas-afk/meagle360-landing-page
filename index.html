FAQ

Questions teams ask before moving their CRM workflow.

A quick overview of how Meagle360 supports lead management, customer operations, and demo requests.

What is Meagle360?

Meagle360 is an enterprise CRM workspace for managing leads, follow-ups, sales pipeline, support cases, customer records, estimates, and invoices.

Can I manage leads and customers in one place?

Yes. Leads, accounts, contacts, opportunities, cases, comments, tasks, files, estimates, and invoices can stay connected to the same customer history.

Can I import existing leads?

Yes. Meagle360 supports lead and customer data workflows such as CSV import and Salesforce import so existing records can move into the CRM.

Does Meagle360 support follow-ups and tasks?

Yes. Teams can assign owners, schedule next actions, add comments, track due dates, and keep follow-up responsibility visible.

Can I manage support cases?

Yes. Support cases, customer conversations, escalations, watchers, approvals, and service history can be tracked with the customer record.

Can I create estimates and invoices?

Yes. Meagle360 includes estimate and invoice workflows so billing can stay connected to the lead, opportunity, account, and customer history.

How do I book a demo?

Use any Book demo, Book walkthrough, or Talk to sales button on this page. Each one opens WhatsApp so you can request a walkthrough directly.
